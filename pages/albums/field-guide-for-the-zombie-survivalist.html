About This Release

The Longwalls' home-brewed debut, Field Guide for the Zombie Survivalist, is a cascading picture book of persons, places and things--living and undead. With cinematic songs about redheads, zombies, God, painkillers, good jobs and really bad ones, The Longwalls stalk a line from classic indie rock to big-twang Americana––with a few heady stops along the way.
